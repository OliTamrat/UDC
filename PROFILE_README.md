# Hi, I'm Oli

Full-stack software engineer building tools that solve real problems — from environmental data platforms to enterprise project management.

## What I'm Working On

### [UDC Water Resources Dashboard](https://github.com/OliTamrat/UDC)
Interactive water quality monitoring platform for the University of the District of Columbia's Water Resources Research Institute. Tracks dissolved oxygen, pH, turbidity, E. coli, and nutrient levels across 12 monitoring stations in the Anacostia River watershed.

**Built with:** Next.js 16 | TypeScript | Leaflet | Recharts | SQLite/PostgreSQL | AI Research Assistant (Claude)

### [Onekof Platform](https://github.com/OliTamrat/onekof-platform)
Enterprise-grade project management platform designed for Ethiopian government agencies, NGOs, and enterprises. Combines Jira-style issue tracking with budget management, team collaboration, and AI-powered document processing.

**Built with:** Next.js | TypeScript | AI Document Processing | Multilingual (Amharic, Oromo, Tigrinya)
